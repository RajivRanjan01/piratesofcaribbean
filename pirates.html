Pirates of the Caribbean

Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl (2003)

In the first film, Captain Jack Sparrow joins forces with blacksmith Will Turner and the governor's daughter, Elizabeth Swann, to rescue Elizabeth from the cursed crew of the Black Pearl, led by Captain Hector Barbossa.

Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest (2006)

In the second installment, Captain Jack Sparrow owes a debt to the legendary Davy Jones and must find a way to escape his grasp while avoiding the East India Trading Company. Meanwhile, Will Turner and Elizabeth Swann seek Jack's help to free him from his debt.

Pirates of the Caribbean: At World's End (2007)

The third film follows Captain Jack Sparrow and his allies as they unite the pirate lords to fight against the East India Trading Company and the supernatural Flying Dutchman, captained by Davy Jones.

Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides (2011)

In this standalone adventure, Captain Jack Sparrow crosses paths with the infamous pirate Blackbeard and his daughter Angelica. They embark on a quest to find the Fountain of Youth, encountering various challenges and rivals along the way.

Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales (2017)

The fifth film sees Captain Jack Sparrow facing off against the vengeful Captain Armando Salazar and his ghostly crew. Jack teams up with Henry Turner, the son of Will Turner, and Carina Smyth, an astronomer, as they search for the Trident of Poseidon to break Salazar's curse.
